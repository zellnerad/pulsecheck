Hey team 👋

As the founding team — the habits we set now become the org the next person walks into. This survey is a gut-check on how we work together today, and what we want to keep, change, or build as we grow. No wrong answers — the more candid, the better.

Our north star

Ship fast on features that create real impact. Everyone on the team should have (1) the context on why we're doing what we're doing, (2) the tools to measure whether it worked, (3) the degrees of freedom to plan and execute their own work, and (4) the space to learn from each other and deliver work they're proud of.

Curious where you think we're living up to that — and where we're not.

Takes ~5 minutes. Answer any or all.
